What is your BMI means?

Body mass index (BMI) is a value derived from the weight and height of a person. If you measure your BMI, you can determine your healthy weight range. If your BMI is:

  • Under 18.5: You are a very underweight person.
  • Between 18.5 to 25.9: You have a healthy weight range for young and middle-aged adults.
  • 25.0 to 29.9: You are overweight.
